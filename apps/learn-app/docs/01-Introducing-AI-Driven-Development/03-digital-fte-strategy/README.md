# Chapter 3: The Digital FTE Strategy

In Chapter 1, you discovered the Agent Factory paradigm—how General Agents build Custom Agents, and how Custom Agents become Digital FTEs that generate recurring revenue. You understand the transformation from coder to orchestrator and why specifications are the new syntax.

Now comes the strategic question: **How do YOU build a Digital FTE business?**

This chapter transforms conceptual understanding into actionable business strategy. You'll learn how to position your unique expertise as a sellable product, navigate competitive dynamics, choose the right monetization model, and avoid the pitfalls that derail most AI projects.

Through eleven interconnected lessons, you'll master the frameworks that separate successful Digital FTE builders from those who remain perpetual tool users.

## Prerequisites

Before starting this chapter, you should have completed:

- **Chapter 1: The Agent Factory Paradigm** — You understand General vs Custom Agents, the Digital FTE vision, and Spec-Driven Development fundamentals
- **Chapter 2: AIFF Foundation** (concurrent) — Standards and patterns for AI-first development

## Chapter Philosophy

This chapter is **strategic and conceptual**—focused on business frameworks rather than coding. The goal is to transform your understanding of what's economically possible with Digital FTEs before diving into technical implementation in later parts.

Every lesson includes a **Try With AI** section where you'll apply frameworks to your own domain and expertise.

## Success Criteria

After completing this chapter, you should be able to:

1. **Articulate your monetization strategy** — Which of the four models fits your domain, with concrete pricing
2. **Map your competitive position** — Where you stand on the Snakes & Ladders layers and your next move
3. **Describe SKILL.md format** — How Agent Skills become portable, licensable intellectual property
4. **Identify deployment red flags** — When NOT to use AI agents and why
5. **Outline your PPP journey** — Your personal Piggyback → Protocol → Pivot market entry path
