## Chapter 1 Lessons Applied

**Simplicity Principles**:
- ✅ All bar/timeline charts limited to 3 data points maximum (Chapter 1 evidence: 3 bars succeed, 5+ fail)
- ✅ Compound words hyphenated proactively in prompts (e.g., "Auto-Complete" → avoid "Autocomplete" misspelling)
- ✅ Arrow/icon indicators text-free (avoid text on visual elements, use titles/captions instead)
- ✅ Labels simplified to essential only (dates/numbers on elements, descriptions separate)

**Redundancy Elimination**:
- ✅ Cross-lesson redundancy check performed (no duplicate teaching goals across chapter)
- ✅ Markdown tables NOT converted to visuals (tables already visual, skip redundant images)

**Pedagogical Value Standards**:
- ✅ All visuals TEACH concepts, not just SHOW data
- ✅ All visuals have one-sentence teaching goals (message test passed)
- ✅ All visuals align with constitutional principles (co-learning, graduated teaching)
