## Success Criteria Validation

### SC-001: Non-Technical Reader Comprehension
**Target**: Non-technical reader can explain 4 concepts

- [ ] Can explain "Specs are the new syntax" concept in their own words
- [ ] Can explain why now is the best time to learn (at least 2 barriers dissolved)
- [ ] Can explain how AI makes them more valuable (not replaceable)
- [ ] Can confirm they qualify for this book (self-identification)

**Validation Method**: Interview with Student/Beginner beta reader  
**Pass Threshold**: All 4 concepts explained clearly

### SC-002: Experienced Developer Comprehension
**Target**: Developer can articulate 3 professional concepts

- [ ] Can distinguish Assisted/Driven/Native approaches with examples
- [ ] Can explain why their expertise is MORE valuable with AI (economic argument)
- [ ] Can articulate "specs are the new syntax" as a professional skill (not remedial)

**Validation Method**: Interview with Developer beta reader  
**Pass Threshold**: All 3 concepts articulated professionally  
**Status**: ⏳ Pending Phase 6 validation

### SC-003: Completion Rate
**Target**: At least 80% of readers complete preface

- [ ] Word count: 4,000-5,000 (appropriate length for completion)
- [ ] Pacing: Smooth transitions between sections (no drag)
- [ ] Engagement: Compelling throughout (no "skippable" sections)

**Validation Method**: Beta reader survey + analytics (if digital)  
**Pass Threshold**: 80% report finishing preface, <5,000 words  
**Status**: ⏳ Pending Phase 6 validation

### SC-004: Welcoming and Inspiring Tone
**Target**: 85% positive sentiment

- [ ] Readers report feeling welcomed (not intimidated)

**Validation Method**: Beta reader sentiment survey  
**Pass Threshold**: 85% positive sentiment (3-4 out of 4 beta readers)  
**Status**: ⏳ Pending Phase 6 validation

**Validation Method**: Beta reader persona identification test  
**Pass Threshold**: All 4 beta readers clearly identify their persona  
**Status**: ⏳ Pending Phase 5 validation
